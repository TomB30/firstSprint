How To Play?

Welcome to MineSweeper!
To win the game you have to flag all the mines, and reveal all the cells without mines.
To flag/unflag a cell - use right click.
To reveal a cell - use left click.
This is a special edition of the game:
1) There is 3 levels:
- 4X4 16 Cells.
- 8X8 64 Cells.
- 12X12 144 Cells.
2) You Have 3 lifes - so you can click 3 bombs before you lose.
3) You have 3 safe clicks - this will mark a cell for you that is safe to click.
4) You have 3 hints - when you click on a hint button , it lets you click on a cell you choose and shows you all the 8 cells around for 2 seconds
